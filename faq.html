Q: What software do you use?

A: Music: Mixcraft 7 Home Studio
Song/album artwork: paint.net and sometimes Audacity (don't ask)
Web editing: Notepad Sublime Text 3, Github Desktop for Windows


Q: More specifically, how do you make the songs in Mixcraft?
A: Currently, all the music I've posted was sequenced by hand on the program's piano roll. I recently got a MIDI controller keyboard, but haven't yet released any music made with it.


Q:Can I use your music in my project?
A: Feel free to do anything non-commercial with the music available for download! I would appreciate credit, though. If you're wondering, Youtube's content ID system won't bite you for using it in a video.


Q: Do you have a tweetbook-facespace-linktube+?
A: Right now, I have a Youtube channel and a website with an RSS feed. Subscribing to either will give you the same notifications about new things and the occasional announcement.
